Official Announcement

Enkyra Public Beta Launch

Released on April 17, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the public beta launch of Enkyra, our next-generation encryption platform designed with innovative technology to transform how you protect your data.

After months of development and private testing, we're excited to invite users to experience the power and simplicity of Enkyra. This beta release marks a significant milestone in our mission to make advanced encryption accessible to everyone.

What's Included in the Beta

  • AES-256 encryption for maximum security
  • Intuitive user interface for easy encryption/decryption
  • Secure storage for encrypted messages
  • Comprehensive history tracking
  • Cross-platform compatibility

Beta Program Details

The public beta will run for approximately 8 weeks, during which we'll be collecting feedback, fixing bugs, and implementing new features based on user suggestions. Your participation helps shape the future of Enkyra.
